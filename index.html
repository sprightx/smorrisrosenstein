How I use AI

My current focus is on building systems and workflows to deliver more work and of a higher quality, faster. My job isn't to manually write every piece of ad copy, every battlecard, and every deck. My job is to be the human in the loop, defining the strategic direction, managing a team of agents through execution, and acting as the expert editor who owns the last mile, taking outputs from first draft to market-ready.

The key to enabling this is CONTEXT. The more robust and exhaustive the context layer, the better AI can generate assets that are credible, high quality, and aligned with the voice, technical specifications, and strategic narrative of the business.
