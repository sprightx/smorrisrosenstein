How I use AI

My job isn't to manually write every piece of ad copy, every battlecard, and every deck. My job is to be the human in the loop that defines the strategic direction, manages a team of agents through execution, and acts as the expert editor owning the last mile, taking an output from first draft to market-ready asset.

The key to enabling this is context. The more exhaustive the context layer, the better AI can generate assets that are credible, high quality, and aligned with the voice, technical specifications, and strategic narrative of the business.
